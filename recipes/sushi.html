Spicy Tuna Sushi Roll

Descritpion

A great tasting spicy roll, for those whi like extra pizzazz. You can use cooked or rav tuna to your preference to achieve great flavors. Great for a filling Japanese meal. Tastes great with a wasabi soy

Ingredients
  • 2 cups uncooked glutinous white rice
  • 2 ½ cups water
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 (5 ounce) can solid white tuna in water, drained
  • 1 tablespoon mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon wasabi paste
  • 4 sheets nori (dry seaweed)
  • ½ cucumber, finely diced
  • carrot, finely diced
  • avocado - peeled, pitted and diced
Directions
    Step 1
  1. Bring the rice, water, and vinegar to a boil in a saucepan over high heat. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until the rice is tender, and the liquid has been absorbed, 20 to 25 minutes. Let stand, covered, for about 10 minutes to absord any excess water, Set rice aside to cool.
    2. Step 2
  2. Lightly mix together the tuna, mayonnaise, chili powder, and wasabi paste in a bowl, breaking th etuna apart bu not mashing it into a paste.
    3. Step 3
  3. To roll the sushi, cover a bamboo sushi rolling mat with plastic wrap. Lay a sheet of nori, rough side up, on the plastic wrap. With wet fingers, firmly pat a thick, even layer of prepared rice over the nori, covering it completely. Place about 1 tablespoon eacheach of diced cucumber, carrot, and avacado in a line along the bottom edge of the sheet, and spread a line of tuna mixture alongside the vegetables.
    4. Step 4
  4. Pick up the edge of the bamboo rolling sheet, fold the bottom edge of the sheet up, enclosing the filling, and tightly roll the sushi into thick cyclinder. Once the sushi is rolled, wrap it inn the mat and gently squeeze to copact it tighlty. Cut each roll into 6 pieces, and refrigerate unitl served.
